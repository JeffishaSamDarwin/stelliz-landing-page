Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in the heart of Geneva in 1892, STELLIZ has been crafting exceptional timepieces for over a century. Our master watchmakers combine traditional Swiss craftsmanship with innovative design to create watches that transcend generations.

Each STELLIZ watch undergoes 847 individual quality checks, ensuring perfection in every detail. From the precise movement of our in-house calibers to the meticulous finishing of every component, excellence is our only standard.
